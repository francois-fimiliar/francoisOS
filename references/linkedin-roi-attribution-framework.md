# LinkedIn ROI Attribution Framework

Source: Marvin Sangines (Notus). Flagged as relevant to Fimiliar's attribution gap.

---

## The 4-Layer System

### Layer 1: Base Metrics
Impressions, followers, likes, profile views. LinkedIn's default output. Directional only — no business impact signal on their own.

### Layer 2: Relevance
Are the right people seeing it? Match base metrics to ICP. High impressions from the wrong audience = noise.

### Layer 3: Signal-Based Outreach
ICP engagement becomes a lead list. Track:
- Who from ICP liked/commented/viewed profile
- Warm outreach triggered by those signals
- Whether those conversations convert to booked meetings

This is the layer Fimiliar's engagement + outreach process lives in. **This is where attribution currently has a gap — comment reply → DM → call → deal tracking is not yet built.**

### Layer 4: Ad Attribution (future)
Insight tag, UTM slugs, li_fat_id tracking, backend automation. Connects LinkedIn ad spend to real pipeline. Not relevant until Fimiliar or clients are running paid.

### Bonus: Self-Reported Attribution
Single form field on sales calls: "How did you hear about us?" Often more actionable than dashboards.

---

## Relevance to Fimiliar

- Layer 3 is the core of what Fimiliar delivers — the gap is tracking it end-to-end
- The attribution tab in the Excel tracker (comment reply → DM → call → deal) would close this loop
- Self-reported attribution on client discovery calls is a quick win — low effort, high signal
