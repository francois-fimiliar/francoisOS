# Tommy Clark — LinkedIn Lead Generation Playbook (2026)

Source: YouTube video transcript. Noted as closely matching the Fimiliar model.

---

## Core Thesis

Cold outbound is dead. The best LinkedIn lead gen in 2026 works by **attracting leads**, not chasing them. Build a content flywheel, design your audience deliberately, engage outbound to warm people up, then DM off intent signals.

---

## The Four Components

### 1. Content Strategy (Founder-Led / Content-Like-GTM)

- Post 5x/week. Cadence: **1 top-of-funnel / 3 middle-of-funnel / 1 bottom-of-funnel**
- Generic tips and tricks are saturated — lean into **personal stories, opinions, and hot takes**
- Post content only *you* can create. AI can copy formatting and style; it can't copy lived experience
- Real images alongside posts prove it's human — builds relatability
- Examples: Austin Hughes (Unifi), Adam Robinson, Matt Stinson (Starbridge)

### 2. Profile as Landing Page

- Clean headshot (no selfies, no AI)
- Headline: role + one-line value prop for followers
- About section: short, human-written — story, what you do now, social proof + CTA
- Featured links: 1–3 max, make the next step obvious (demo booking + newsletter/lead magnet)
- Custom button (LinkedIn Premium): direct to highest-priority CTA

### 3. Audience Design

- Send targeted outbound connection requests to ICP — when accepted, they automatically follow you and enter your content flywheel
- Bypasses algorithm dependency early on
- Lets you seed your audience with the right people from day one

### 4. Outbound Engagement

- Comment on ICP content and on creators your ICP follows
- Comments are content — visibility in the right comment sections builds recognition
- Reciprocal engagement from other creators amplifies your posts

---

## Warm Outbound (DMs)

Once the flywheel is running, DMs aren't cold anymore — they're warm.

**Intent signals to trigger outreach:**
- Profile views (check who's looking at your profile)
- Post engagement (people liking/commenting who fit ICP and aren't connected yet)

Message someone after a signal = they've already seen you. Conversion rates are materially higher.

---

## Why Cold Outbound Alone Fails

- People ignore canned, templated DMs regardless of personalization
- No know/like/trust foundation
- Fighting an uphill battle even with good targeting
