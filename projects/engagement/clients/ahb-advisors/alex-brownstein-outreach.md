# Outreach Strategy — Alex Brownstein

*Company: AHB Advisors*
*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Philosophy

Alex's DMs should feel like they come from a peer — someone who has been in the same rooms, read the same reports, and has a specific reason for reaching out now. The bar is high: Alex is analytically sharp and will immediately see through anything generic. Every DM must reference exactly what the prospect said or did.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

---

## Intent Threshold

Minimum: 2-3 engagement touches from Alex + one awareness signal.

| Signal | Intent | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Replied to Alex's comment | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Commented on Alex's post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Sent unprompted connection request | High | DM within 48hrs |
| Viewed Alex's profile after interaction | High | DM within 72hrs |
| Liked 2+ Alex posts same week | Medium | Wait for next signal |
| Referenced Alex's Marketecture contribution | Very high | DM within 48hrs |

**Additional signal to watch:** anyone engaging with Alex's Marketecture content or referencing it — they already know his work.

---

## Message Framework

### Message 1

**Voice:** Dry, precise, analytical. Stated as fact, never performed. McKinsey-style directness. No warm-up phrases.

**Topics Alex can credibly open on:**
- Retail media monetisation and publisher economics
- Programmatic supply chain and SPO
- PE value creation in media/adtech portfolios
- Ad-tech M&A and market structure
- CTV and streaming monetisation
- Publisher revenue strategy

**Example opener structure:**
> "[Reference exactly what they said — their specific take, number, or argument.]
> [One short, precise observation that adds to or interrogates their point — data-led if possible.]
> [One question. Analytical, genuinely curious. Not leading toward a pitch.]"

**Rules:**
- Under 60 words / under 300 characters
- One idea only — no multi-part messages
- Data or specificity over generality
- No AHB Advisors mention in Message 1
- No "would love to connect", no "great post", no performed enthusiasm
- Draw from what they actually said — not the topic generally

---

### Message 2 (if no reply, 5-7 days later)

Different angle. Ideally triggered by something they posted after Message 1.

> "[New reference point — something they posted or shared.]
> [Short additional observation or a different analytical angle.]
> [Different question.]"

**Hard limit: 2 DMs without a reply.**

---

## Voice Rules

**Use:**
- Precise language — "the 40% take rate shift" not "significant changes in pricing"
- Well-sourced references where relevant
- Dry, confident tone — not warm, not corporate

**Avoid:**
- Em dashes
- "Sit with", "unpack", "explore"
- Performed surprise or enthusiasm
- Rhetorical questions as openers
- Mentioning competitors
- Politics or religion
- Generic adtech/media commentary that anyone could have written

---

## No-Go List

- Cold DMs
- Pitch in Messages 1 or 2
- Calendar link before real conversation exists
- Generic personalisation ([NAME] swap only)
- Any reference to linked articles (only draw from the post itself)
- More than 2 DMs without a reply

---

## Strongest Prospect Signals for Alex

Given Alex's six ICP segments, highest-value signals are:
- CROs and CEOs at sub-scale publishers replying to his comments on programmatic/monetisation content
- PE operating partners engaging with his retail media or M&A takes
- AdTech founders commenting on Marketecture content (already aware of Alex's work)
- Anyone at a KKR/Vista/Thoma Bravo portfolio company engaging with his content
