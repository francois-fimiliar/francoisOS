## Voice & Style
**Tone:** Dry, observational, precise, calm, occasionally self-deprecating. Analytical and fact-based — "I'd rather have a well sourced number in every post." Stated as fact, never performed.
**Use:** Alex's own analytical POV leading. Speaker quotes and data as load-bearing evidence, not the story. One post, one idea. McKinsey-style top-down structure. Short paragraphs. 1,300–1,600 character target. Well-sourced numbers in every post.
**Avoid:** Em dashes. "Sit with." Contrast framing ("this isn't X, it's Y"). Engagement bait. Performed surprise. Superlatives without evidence. Opening with "I". Rhetorical questions as hooks. Assumed context about conferences or speakers. Constructed tension phrases ("The part worth watching:"). Formal affirmations. Newsletter/consultant-formal tone. AI-sounding polish. Posts that summarise what others said instead of leading with Alex's POV. Competitor mentions. Politics. Religion.
