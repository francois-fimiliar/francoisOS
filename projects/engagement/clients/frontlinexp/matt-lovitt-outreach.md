## Intent Threshold

Minimum: 2-3 engagement touches from Matt + one awareness signal from the prospect.

| Signal | Intent | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Replied to Matt's comment | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Commented on Matt's post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Sent unprompted connection request | High | DM within 48hrs |
| Viewed Matt's profile after interaction | High | DM within 72hrs |
| Liked 2+ Matt posts same week | Medium | Wait for next signal |
| Appeared in same comment section 2+ times | Medium | Wait for next signal |

**Coordination rule:** Check Rob Bate's prospect list before DMing. Never DM the same person on the same day from both Matt and Rob.
