## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | Operational angle that extends the post | "The part that gets missed here is..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect to frontline transformation reality | "Seen this at enterprise retail/hospitality scale..." |
| Contrarian | Push back on generic workforce tech takes | "Agree with most of this, but the implementation reality is..." |
| Question | Invite dialogue with ICP prospects | "Curious how this plays out when you're scheduling across 500+ sites..." |
| Amplify | Strengthen the best point in the post | "The [specific point] is underrated — most operators underestimate..." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- Every comment adds a perspective, a question, or a real-world example.
- One comment per person per day max.
- No promotional language. Never name FrontlineXP unprompted.
- Sound like a practitioner — not a vendor.
- Differentiator to signal organically: independent, cross-platform expertise. No vendor bias.
