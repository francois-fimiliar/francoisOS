# Outreach Strategy — Andy Newberry

*Company: Legion Technologies UK*
*Sector: Retail & Hospitality*
*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Philosophy

Andy's DMs reach a buying committee — the decision to buy Legion is made by Ops, Finance, and HR together. His messages need to resonate across all three functions without being written for all three simultaneously. Pick the angle relevant to the prospect's role, and trust that the problem (right people, right place, right time) is shared across the committee.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

---

## Message Framework

**Angle by prospect role:**

- Seasonal demand planning (Christmas, summer, promotions)

**Example opener structure:**
> "[Reference their specific post or comment — exact.]

**Rules:**
- Under 60 words / under 300 characters
- Match the angle to the prospect's role
- Never pitch Legion in Message 1
- Andy's ICP = retail and hospitality only — never cross into FM (Rob's territory)
- No calendar link
