**Core problem Legion solves for this ICP:**
The gap between how many people a business needs and how work actually happens on the frontline. Companies either schedule too many people (wasting money) or too few (hurting service, burning out staff), then spend the day firefighting. Compound factors: demand changes by hour or day (peaks, seasonality, promotions, weather, events), high frontline churn driven by unpredictable hours, multi-site coordination at scale.

**Value prop:** "Legion helps retailers and hospitality operators put the right people in the right place at the right time, reducing labour waste while giving frontline teams fairer, more predictable schedules."
