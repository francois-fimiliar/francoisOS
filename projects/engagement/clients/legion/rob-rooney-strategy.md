## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | FM-specific operational angle | "The agency dependency problem in FM is particularly sharp because..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect to FM operational reality at scale | "Seen this in security services at multi-site FM contracts — the rota problem compounds fast." |
| Contrarian | Push back on generic FM/workforce management takes | "The scheduling piece is usually blamed, but the real driver is demand forecasting..." |
| Question | Invite dialogue from FM operations leaders | "Curious how this plays out when your cleaning contract adds a site at 48 hours' notice..." |
| Amplify | Strengthen the most FM-relevant point | "The retention piece especially — unpredictable hours are the number one driver of churn in security and cleaning." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- FM-sector specificity always — not generic WFM commentary.
- Never pitch Legion directly in a comment.
- Sound like an FM operations expert, not a software AE.
- Rob's ICP = FM exclusively. Never drift into retail/hospitality (Andy's territory).
