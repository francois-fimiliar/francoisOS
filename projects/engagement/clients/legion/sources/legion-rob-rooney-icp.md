What is the core problem you solve for them?: Facilities management and hospitality businesses run on people — and those people work in shifts.

The core problem is that coordinating large, dispersed, hourly workforces across multiple sites is operationally complex and expensive. Demand fluctuates constantly — a hotel fills up, a cleaning contract expands, a security post needs covering at short notice — and the default response is to either overstaff as a buffer or scramble for agency workers at premium cost.

On top of that, the people doing this work — cleaners, security staff, porters, housekeepers, catering teams — are among the hardest to retain. Unpredictable hours and a lack of control over their schedule is one of the primary reasons they leave.

Legion solves both sides of that equation. It gives businesses the intelligence to forecast demand accurately and schedule the right people automatically, cutting waste and removing the agency dependency. And it gives workers visibility and flexibility over their own hours, which directly reduces the turnover that costs these industries so much time and money.

The net result — leaner labour costs, more stable teams, managers focused on running the operation rather than filling rotas, and a workforce that actually shows up.
