**Core problem Legion solves for this ICP:**
Coordinating large, dispersed, hourly workforces across multiple sites — demand fluctuates constantly (hotel fills up, cleaning contract expands, security post needs covering at short notice) and the default response is overstaffing or expensive agency workers. Compound problem: cleaners, security staff, porters, housekeepers are among the hardest to retain — unpredictable hours drive turnover. Legion solves both sides: accurate demand forecasting + automated scheduling cuts agency dependency; worker visibility and flexibility over hours reduces turnover.
