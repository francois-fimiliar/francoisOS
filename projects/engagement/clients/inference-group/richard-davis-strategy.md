## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | Practical angle that extends the AI/transformation post | "The piece that doesn't get written enough is what happens between PoC and production..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect to UK enterprise AI delivery reality | "Seen this specifically in FSI/retail/energy at UK enterprise scale..." |
| Contrarian | Push back on hype with delivery specificity | "The ROI framing is right, but the delivery assumption usually isn't..." |
| Question | Invite dialogue from CTO/CIO/CDO prospects | "Curious how this changes when your governance requirement is UK/EU regulation..." |
| Amplify | Strengthen the most practically useful point | "The PoC-to-production gap is the real problem — and it's rarely technical..." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- Every comment grounds AI in delivery reality — not theory.
- Never pitch Inference Group unprompted.
- UK enterprise specificity is a differentiator — use it.
- Sound like a CEO who delivers, not a consultant who advises.
