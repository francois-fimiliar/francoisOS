# Outreach Strategy — Richard Davis

*Company: Inference Group*
*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Philosophy

Richard's DMs come from a CEO who has delivered enterprise AI — not from someone who theorises about it. The ICP (UK enterprise: FSI, retail, energy, telcos) is sophisticated and sceptical of AI hype. Every message needs to be grounded in delivery reality, not vision statements.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

---

## Intent Threshold

Minimum: 2-3 engagement touches from Richard + one awareness signal.

| Signal | Intent | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Replied to Richard's comment | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Commented on Richard's post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Sent unprompted connection request | High | DM within 48hrs |
| Viewed Richard's profile after interaction | High | DM within 72hrs |
| Liked 2+ Richard posts same week | Medium | Wait for next signal |
| Posted about AI ROI, PoC failure, or shadow AI | Very high | DM within 48hrs — pain point active |

**Highest-value signal:** prospect posting about AI disappointment ("we bought Copilot but aren't seeing value", "our PoC didn't scale") — this is an active pain signal, DM immediately.

**Rules:**

---

### Message 2 (if no reply, 5-7 days later)

Different angle. New signal if possible. Still grounded in delivery, not theory.

**Hard limit: 2 DMs without a reply.**

---

## Voice Rules

**Use:** CEO delivery voice — what actually happens in enterprise AI projects, not what should happen
**Avoid:** AI hype language, buzzwords ("agentic", "transformative"), Big 4 consulting framing, anything that sounds vendor-pitched

---

## No-Go List

- Cold DMs
- AI hype in any message
- Pitch or product mention in Messages 1 or 2
- Calendar link before conversation opens
- More than 2 DMs without a reply
- Non-UK prospects (Richard's ICP is UK-only)

---

## Strongest Prospect Signals for Richard

- CTOs/CIOs/CDOs at UK FSI, retail, energy, or telco companies posting about AI challenges
- Anyone using language like "PoC", "shadow AI", "Copilot adoption", "AI governance"
- Digital transformation leaders expressing frustration with AI ROI
- Head of AI or Head of Transformation roles engaging with his content
