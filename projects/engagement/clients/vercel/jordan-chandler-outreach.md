# Outreach Strategy — Jordan Chandler

*Company: Vercel*
*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Philosophy

Jordan's DMs must never read like a sales rep reaching out. The strategy pivot is away from sales-heavy content toward technical credibility — the DM has to match that. Every message should feel like it comes from a technically literate peer, not an AE with a quota.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

---

## Intent Threshold

Minimum: 2-3 engagement touches from Jordan + one awareness signal.

| Signal | Intent | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Replied to Jordan's comment | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Commented on Jordan's post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Sent unprompted connection request | High | DM within 48hrs |
| Viewed Jordan's profile after interaction | High | DM within 72hrs |
| Liked 2+ Jordan posts same week | Medium | Wait for next signal |
| GCC-based prospect engaging with Jordan's content | High | DM with regional angle within 48hrs |

**GCC note:** Jordan's regional expertise (UKI + GCC) is an underused differentiator. A CTO in Dubai or Riyadh receiving a DM from someone who specifically covers their market is far more likely to engage.

---

## Message Framework

### Message 1

**Voice:** Technically literate, not sales-y. Peer-to-peer. Never pitching.

**Topics Jordan can credibly open on:**
- Frontend infrastructure decisions at enterprise scale
- Developer experience and engineering team productivity
- Next.js and React ecosystem in production
- Edge computing trade-offs
- UKI engineering and developer tooling landscape
- GCC digital transformation and developer ecosystem growth

**Example opener structure:**
> "[Reference exactly what they said or did — their specific technical take or question.]
> [One technically specific observation — not a product reference, a genuine engineering perspective.]
> [One question — about their stack, their team's experience, or their market context.]"

**Rules:**
- Under 60 words / under 300 characters
- Technical depth always — never pitch Vercel
- GCC prospects: add regional specificity ("in the GCC market specifically...")
- No "would love to show you what we're building"
- No calendar link in Messages 1 or 2

---

### Message 2 (if no reply, 5-7 days later)

Different technical angle. New reference point if possible.

**Hard limit: 2 DMs without a reply.**

---

## Voice Rules

**Use:** Technical specificity, regional context, engineering peer framing
**Avoid:** Sales language, product pitches, "I'd love to learn more about your challenges", generic enterprise SaaS opener language

---

## No-Go List

- Cold DMs
- Mentioning Vercel as a pitch in Messages 1 or 2
- Calendar link before a real conversation
- Generic "I noticed you're a CTO" openers
- More than 2 DMs without a reply

## Strongest Prospect Signals for Jordan

- CTOs/VP Engineering at enterprise SaaS companies in UKI replying to his technical comments
- Engineering leaders at GCC companies engaging with his content (high-value, underserved market)
- Anyone discussing frontend infrastructure decisions or DX investment
