## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | Commercial angle on LinkedIn personal branding | "The part that gets missed in the personal branding conversation is the pipeline side..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect to Fimiliar client outcomes or commercial results | "Seen this pattern play out consistently with the founders we work with..." |
| Contrarian | Push back on vanity metrics or content-first framing | "Follower count is the wrong metric. The question is always ICP composition in the audience..." |
| Question | Invite dialogue from founders/sales leaders | "Curious whether this changes when your LinkedIn activity is tied to a named account list..." |
| Amplify | Strengthen the most commercially useful point | "The warm outbound piece here is underrated — consistency in engagement is what turns cold into warm." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- Pipeline and commercial outcomes framing — not growth hacks.
- Never position Fimiliar as a content agency.
- Sound like a founder who has built the system, not a coach who recommends it.

## Prospect Targeting

**ICP:** Founders, CEOs, SaaS sales leaders (VPs, Directors), consulting firm partners
**Industries:** SaaS, B2B tech, consulting
**Geography:** UK, US, Middle East (Fimiliar primary markets)

**How to identify posts to comment on:**
- Posts about: LinkedIn personal branding, warm outbound, pipeline generation, B2B sales, LinkedIn strategy
- Founders/sales leaders expressing frustration with cold outreach, LinkedIn ROI, or personal brand building
- People in connect/warm queue — prioritise first
- Intent signals: "LinkedIn isn't working for us," "how do I build pipeline from LinkedIn," "personal brand ROI"

**Avoid:**
- Consumer/B2C content
- Generic business motivation/inspiration without LinkedIn or pipeline angle
- Content from companies/individuals clearly outside Fimiliar's market size
