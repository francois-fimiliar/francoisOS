# Outreach Strategy — Francois van Zyl

*Company: Fimiliar (Internal)*
*Last updated: 2026-04-29*

---

## Philosophy

Francois's DMs come from someone who runs the engagement function — not someone recommending it from the outside. He tests strategies on himself before applying to clients. That operational credibility is the hook. Every message should feel like it's from a practitioner who noticed something specific about how they're running (or not running) their LinkedIn engagement, not a consultant pitching a service.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

## Message Framework

### Message 1

**Voice:** Direct, operational, growth-focused. No fluff. Systems thinking. Real examples from running engagement at scale.

**Topics Francois can credibly open on:**
- LinkedIn engagement operations — what it actually looks like running it across multiple profiles
- Warm outbound — how intent signals change who you reach out to and when
- ICP composition in your LinkedIn audience — the metric most people ignore
- Comment strategy and why volume doesn't fix a broken ICP
- Operational leverage in growth functions

**Example opener structure:**
> "[Reference their specific post or comment — exact observation.]
> [One operational angle from running engagement at scale — 'what we see consistently when running this across client profiles...']
> [One direct question about their situation.]"

**Rules:**
- Under 60 words / under 300 characters
- Engagement/ops framing — distinct from Karl (pipeline) and Jono (creative)
- Test-and-learn framing works: "testing this on myself before rolling to clients"
- No pitch in Message 1
- No Fimiliar mention until conversation has opened naturally
- No calendar link before real conversation exists

---

### Message 2 (if no reply, 5-7 days later)

Different operational angle. New reference if possible.

**Hard limit: 2 DMs without a reply.**

---

## Voice Rules

**Use:** Operational specificity, systems thinking, practitioner framing, direct language
**Avoid:** Fluff, AI-sounding polish, generic LinkedIn advice, coaching language, content agency framing

---

## No-Go List

- Cold DMs
- Pitch in Messages 1 or 2
- Fimiliar mention before conversation has opened
- Calendar link before real conversation
- More than 2 DMs without a reply
- ICP composition stats that are unverified (standing flag — no unconfirmed percentages in any external message)

---

## Strongest Prospect Signals for Francois

- SaaS VPs/sales leaders posting about outbound ROI, LinkedIn not converting, or engagement systems
- Consulting partners trying to build their personal brand and not getting traction
- Series A–C founders asking how to turn LinkedIn activity into pipeline
- Anyone expressing frustration with the gap between LinkedIn effort and actual results
