## Philosophy

Jono's angle is creative direction — the craft layer of B2B LinkedIn content. His DMs should feel like they come from someone who cares deeply about brand voice and content quality, not someone selling a service. The hook is always a genuine observation about their content or brand, never a pitch.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

## Voice Rules

**Use:** Creative practitioner framing, specific observations about their content/voice, genuine curiosity
**Avoid:** Marketing strategy language, pipeline framing (that's Karl), coaching language, content agency positioning

## No-Go List

- Pitch in Messages 1 or 2
- Pipeline/commercial framing (Karl's lane)
- Content agency positioning
- Calendar link before conversation opens
- More than 2 DMs without a reply

- Founders/marketing leaders posting about content strategy or brand voice
