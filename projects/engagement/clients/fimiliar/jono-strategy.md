## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|

| Experience bridge | Connect to content production or creative direction experience | "Seen this gap between strategy and execution kill otherwise good LinkedIn plans..." |
| Contrarian | Push back on content quantity over quality | "More posts rarely fixes the problem — the issue is usually the voice isn't differentiated enough." |

| Amplify | Strengthen the most creative/brand-specific point | "The distinctiveness point is underrated — on LinkedIn, forgettable is the same as invisible." |
