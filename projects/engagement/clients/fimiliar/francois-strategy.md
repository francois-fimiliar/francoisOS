## Voice & Tone

**Tone:** Direct, operational, growth-focused. No fluff. Authentic founder/operator voice — not polished personal branding.
**Use:** Systems thinking, operational leverage, execution quality. Real examples from client work and operations. Content pillars: Customer proof/observation, Education/frameworks, Industry insights.
**Avoid:** Fluff. Excessive thought leadership without substance. AI-sounding polish. Generic content that any LinkedIn consultant could have written. Content that positions Fimiliar as a content agency.

## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | Engagement or outbound systems angle | "The operational piece here is what usually breaks first at scale..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect to real client engagement or outbound work | "Running this daily across a dozen client profiles — the pattern I keep seeing is..." |
| Contrarian | Push back on volume-first or vanity metric takes | "More activity doesn't fix a broken ICP composition. The comments are going to the wrong people." |
| Question | Invite dialogue from sales leaders and founders | "Curious whether this holds when you're running outbound at scale across multiple accounts simultaneously..." |
| Amplify | Strengthen the most operationally specific point | "The consistency piece especially — that's the part that's hardest to maintain and most correlated to results." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- Engagement and outbound operations angle — distinct from Karl (pipeline/commercial) and Jono (creative direction).
- Test strategies on myself before applying to clients — comments can reflect this.
- Sound like a practitioner running the engagement function, not an advisor recommending it.
- ICP composition quality (not raw engagement numbers) is the right metric to reference.

**ICP:** SaaS sales leaders (VPs, Directors), consulting firm partners, Series A–C founders
**Industries:** SaaS, B2B consulting, professional services
**Geography:** UK, US, Middle East (Fimiliar primary markets)

**How to identify posts to comment on:**
- Posts about: LinkedIn engagement, warm outbound, pipeline, B2B growth, personal brand, sales enablement, operational systems
- Sales leaders/founders talking about LinkedIn ROI, outbound results, or building pipeline
- People in connect/warm queue — prioritise first
- Intent signals: "outbound isn't working," "LinkedIn activity isn't converting," "we need a better engagement system"

**Avoid:**
- Consumer/B2C content
- Non-commercial LinkedIn content (lifestyle, motivation without business angle)
- Content from companies outside Fimiliar's ICP
