## Template 1 — Finding Your Happy Place in the Chaos

Source: Chris Ortega (Fresh FP&A)
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7454955262001537024/

**Format:**
1. Hard truth hook ("I just figured out a hard truth about being a business owner...")
2. Personal image + real moment (Starbucks, back-to-back calls)
3. Contrast: most people do X (love outcomes, hate the chaos)
4. Reframe: I've found my happy place inside the chaos
5. Specifics of what energises you (bullet points with checkmarks)
6. "That is my VIBE"
7. Shift for the reader — what they should do
8. Soft CTA (your job vs. our job)
9. Engagement question at the end

**Why it works:**
- Personal and specific — real image, real moment, genuine emotion
- Relatable tension (chaos of business ownership)
- Clear positioning without a hard pitch
- Ends with a question that invites the audience to share their own version

**Adapt for Ilan:**
- The "happy place" = helping founders navigate the financial complexity of a raise or exit
- The chaos = the M&A process, fundraising prep, financial visibility gaps
- Proof points = specific situations where Ilan comes alive (messy cap tables, pre-exit due diligence, cash visibility crises)
- Avoid: making it sound like a service pitch. Keep it personal and specific to Ilan's experience as a founder.
