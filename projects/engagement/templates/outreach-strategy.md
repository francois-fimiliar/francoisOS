## Philosophy

DMs are never the introduction — they make an existing relationship direct. By the time a DM lands, the prospect already knows who [CLIENT] is from their presence in comment sections. The DM is the natural next step, not a cold open.

**Warm outreach only. No exceptions.**

---

## Intent Threshold

A prospect qualifies for a DM when they have:
1. **Minimum 2-3 engagement touches** from [CLIENT] (comments, likes, profile visits)
2. **At least one awareness signal** from the prospect:

| Signal | Intent | Priority |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Replied to [CLIENT]'s comment on another post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Commented on [CLIENT]'s own post | Very high | DM within 48hrs |
| Sent unprompted connection request | High | DM within 48hrs |
| Viewed [CLIENT]'s profile after interaction | High | DM within 72hrs |
| Liked 2+ [CLIENT] posts in the same week | Medium | DM when next signal fires |
| Appeared in same comment section 2+ times | Medium | DM when next signal fires |

**No signal = stay in comment mode. Don't force it.**

---

## Message Framework

### Message 1 — The opener

**Structure:**
- Line 1: Reference the specific signal (not generic — name the post, the comment, the interaction)
- Lines 2-3: One genuine observation or reaction
- Line 4: One open-ended question

**Rules:**
- Under 60 words / under 300 characters
- One question only
- No CTA, no calendar link, no pitch, no links
- No "I'd love to connect" or "would be great to chat"
- Sound like a person who noticed something, not a sequence that fired
- Send between 8-10am or 2-4pm GMT for highest reply rates

**Template skeleton:**
> [Reference the signal — "Saw your reply on [X's post] about [topic]..."]
> [One honest reaction or observation]
> [One open question that invites a real answer]

---

### Message 2 — Follow-up (if no reply after 5-7 days)

**Rules:**
- Different angle from Message 1 — don't repeat
- Add something: a thought, a resource, a question prompted by something new they posted
- Still no pitch, no CTA
- Under 60 words

**If no reply to Message 2:** Return to warm engagement mode for 3-4 weeks. Only re-DM if a new intent signal fires.

**Hard limit: 2 DMs per sequence without a reply.**

## Voice Rules

[CLIENT]'s DMs should sound like [CLIENT] — pull from the voice/style section in the profile file.

**Universal rules across all clients:**
- No corporate opener ("Hope this finds you well", "I came across your profile")
- No performed enthusiasm ("Love what you're doing!")
- No vague compliment ("Really enjoyed your post")
- Be specific. Reference exactly what they said or did.
- Ask something you'd actually want to know the answer to.

---

## No-Go List

- Cold DMs to anyone who hasn't shown a signal
- Pitching the service in Message 1 or 2
- Calendar links before a conversation exists
- Generic openers with [NAME] swap as the only personalisation
- Following up more than twice without a reply
- DMing someone the same day you first connect (unless they messaged first)
