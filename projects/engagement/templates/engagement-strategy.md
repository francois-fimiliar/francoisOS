## Comment Types — Rotate These

| Type | When to use | Example opener |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Insight add | You have a perspective that extends the post | "The part that often gets missed here is..." |
| Experience bridge | Connect the post to a real-world example | "Seen this firsthand at [context]..." |
| Contrarian | Respectfully challenge a take | "Agree with most of this, but..." |
| Question | Invite dialogue — drives replies and reach | "Curious how this plays out when..." |
| Amplify | Expand on the strongest point | "This point about X is underrated..." |

**Rules:**
- 2–4 sentences minimum. No one-liners.
- Batch to 8–10am local time — early comments on fresh posts get more algorithm visibility.
- Every comment must add something — a perspective, a question, an example.
- One comment per person per day max.
- No promotional language. Ever.
- Sound like a practitioner, not a consultant pitching.
